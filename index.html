Maximizing on-shelf availability (OSA) for CPGs with machine learning

On-shelf availability (OSA) is a crucial metric for consumer packaged goods (CPGs) companies, as it measures the percentage of time that a product is available for sale on the shelves of a retail store. Low OSA can lead to lost sales, reduced customer satisfaction, and damaged brand reputation. Machine learning can help CPGs maximize OSA by predicting and preventing out-of-stock situations.
